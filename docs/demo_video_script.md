# 🎬 ModernTensor: Hackathon Demo Video Guide (3 Minutes)

This script is designed to win the **"AI & Agents"** track. It emphasizes **Trust, Security, and Hedera Consensus**.

## 🎥 Setup
*   **Screen 1 (Left):** Terminal (vs code) ready to run `python scripts/demo_agent_verification.py`.
*   **Screen 2 (Right):** ModernTensor Dashboard (localhost:3000) open in Chrome.
*   **Optional:** HashScan tab open in background.

---

## ⏱️ Scene 1: The Hook (0:00 - 0:45)
**Visual:** Show slide of "The Problem".
**Voiceover:**
"We are entering the Agentic Economy. By 2026, autonomous AI agents will trade our assets, write our code, and manage our lives. But who watches the watchers? If an Agent goes rogue, how do we know?"

**Visual:** Switch to **ModernTensor Dashboard**.
**Voiceover:**
"Introducing **ModernTensor**: The Trust Layer for Autonomous Agents on Hedera."
"We provide a decentralized verification protocol that validates Agent capabilities and logs their 'Trust Score' immutably on the Hedera Consensus Service."

---

## ⏱️ Scene 2: The Core Demo (0:45 - 2:00)
**Action:**
1.  **Start the Script:** Run `python scripts/demo_agent_verification.py` in the terminal.
2.  **Point to Dashboard:** Immediately hover over the **"Active Verification Protocol"** banner that appears at the top.

**Voiceover:**
"Let's watch a live verification. Here, a 'DeFi Trading Agent' is applying for a Verified Badge."
"Step 1: The agent registers on-chain."
*(Wait for script to show 'Protocol issued Challenge')*
"Step 2: ModernTensor automatically issues a **Benchmark Challenge**. In this case, it's a 'Smart Contract Vulnerability Scan' to test the agent's security logic."

**Action:**
*   Terminal shows "Agent submitting solution..."
*   Dashboard updates to show status.

**Voiceover:**
"The Agent solves the challenge in real-time. But we don't just trust it. We verify it."
"Our **Proof of Quality (PoQ)** mechanism uses a committee of Validator Nodes to score the solution."

---

## ⏱️ Scene 3: The "Wow" Factor (HCS) (2:00 - 2:30)
**Action:**
*   Script finishes. Terminal shows `Transaction ID`.
*   Dashboard shows green **"VERIFIED ON HEDERA"** badge.
*   **Click** the HashScan link (or search the Topic ID).

**Voiceover:**
"And here is the magic. The final Trust Score is logged instantly on **Hedera Consensus Service**."
"Why Hedera? Because AI moves at the speed of light. Only Hedera's high throughput can handle the firehose of verification logs required for a global agent economy."

---

## ⏱️ Scene 4: Conclusion (2:30 - 3:00)
**Visual:** Show "Architecture Diagram" or back to Dashboard.
**Voiceover:**
"ModernTensor isn't just a marketplace. It's the **Internal Affairs** department for AI."
"We are building the standard for Safe Agentic AI. Built on Hedera. Thank you."

---

## 📝 Tips
*   **Speak slowly.**
*   **Zoom in** on the Terminal text so judges can read "Trust Score > 0.8".
*   **Don't mention the failed smart contract.** Focus on HCS as the *intended* architecture for speed.

Core Features
A complete AI marketplace with reputation-driven incentives
🏆

Reputation-Weighted Rewards

Validators earn based on accuracy, stake amount, and accumulated reputation. Accurate validators with more skin-in-the-game earn exponentially more.

🌐

Cross-Subnet Portability

Port 50% of your reputation to new subnets. Proven validators are incentivized to participate across multiple AI domains.

⚡

Adaptive Validation

High-value tasks automatically require more validators. Tasks over 10,000 MDT need 4 validators instead of the standard 2.

🔥

Dynamic Registration Fee

EIP-1559-style registration fee that increases during spam and decreases over time. All fees are permanently burned.

💰

Passive Staking Rewards

5% of all task fees go to the staking pool. Miners, validators, and holders all earn passive rewards proportional to their stake.

🛡️

On-Chain Security

ReentrancyGuard, Pausable, CEI pattern. 7-day unstake cooldown. OpenZeppelin-based. Deployed and verified on Hedera.
